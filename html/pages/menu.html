Our Menu

Moore's offer's a Traditional Full Irish Breakfast of Guinness sausages, Clonakilty rashers, black & white pudding, Batchelor's baked beans, local Free Range eggs, tomatoes, mushrooms and home made potato farls.


We also offer a Continental Breakfast with a selection of juices, Cuisine de France pastries, Brown, White Bread and Toast and a selection of jams, spreads and fresh fruit.


We also have a wide selection of Cereals, including Cornflakes, Weetabix, Rice Krispies, Alpen, Porridge freshly made with Fresh Milk, Milk Alternative or Water


Moore's breakfast also caters to a wide range of diets so we offer our Vegetarian Breakfast Omelette and we can accomedate any other dietry restrictions you may have.
