Moore's is a unique family run Bed & Breakfast overlooking Galway Bay on the west coast of Ireland. Based at the heart of Galway City we are the perfect location for anyone wishing to explore Ireland's spectacular Atlantic coastline or Galway's historic sites. Offering luxery quality dining and accommodation at an affordable price Moore's is an ideal choice for couple's or families. We are child friendly and cater for any dietry resttrictions and are wheelchair accessablie so are perfect for anyone no matter your age, tastes or mobility.


All our rooms are luxuriously furnished, non-smoking, own private bathrooms with full size baths, separate walk-in tropical rain showers, walnut wooden floors, central heating, iron and ironing board, hair dryer, all with 32" to 40" flat T.V. and tea/coffee making facilities.


Moore's breakfast offer's a Traditional Full Irish Breakfast of rashers, sausages, black & white pudding, baked beans, eggs, tomatoes, mushrooms and potato farl. We also offer a Continental Breakfast, Vegetarian Breakfast Omelette and a wide range of cereals and we can also cater for any other dietry restrictions you may have.


Moore's is located within walking distance of two sandy beaches offering spectacular coastal walks, horse-riding, golfing, fishing, biking, water sports, as well as a variety of local restaurants and friendly traditional Irish pubs.
