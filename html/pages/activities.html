Moore's is a unique family run Bed & Breakfast overlooking Galway Bay on the west coast of Ireland. Based at the heart of Galway City we are the perfect location for anyone wishing to explore Ireland's spectacular Atlantic coastline or Galway's historic sites.


Moore's is located within walking distance of two sandy beaches offering spectacular coastal walks, horse-riding, golfing, fishing, biking and water sports.


Galway City is also home to a variety of local restaurants, friendly traditional Irish pubs the Eyre Square Shopping Centre.


Sightseeing tours can be arranged at the B&B at a discount for: Connemara , Cliffs of Moher , The Aran Islands , bus tour of Galway City. All tickets can be purchased on arrival.
